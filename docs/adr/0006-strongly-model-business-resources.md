# Strongly Model Business Resources

Event OS strongly models long-lived business resources such as Activity, Organizer, Session, Speaker, Registration, QR Pass, My Agenda, Check-in, Expo Booth, Sponsor, Live Entry, Survey, Notification, and Audit Event. Page Config and Blocks may use JSON for display composition, ordering, copy, styling, and resource references, but they must not become the source of truth for business resources.
